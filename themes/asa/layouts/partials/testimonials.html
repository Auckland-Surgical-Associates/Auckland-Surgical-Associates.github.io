What our patients say

Real stories from people whose lives have been transformed

Last year I made the decision that I needed help to lose weight. It was a battle I had fought for my entire life and that I was slowly losing. I had, had a major mental breakdown that led to a break through in some childhood trauma and that had led me to finally look at myself and realize I needed to change or else I wasn't going to be there for my kids for much longer. I have 5 kids from 21 to 4 years old. The oldest and youngest are both high dependency special needs, so me being fit, health and strong is really important.

From my very first phone call Auckland surgical associates was absolutely amazing. Friendly, helpful and welcoming. They made absolutely everything easy and stress free, explaining everything in ways I could easy understand. Dr Suheelan, Maddie and the rest of the team have been so helpful with any questions that I have had. Both pre and post operation. Txts and calls answered and replied to really promptly.

I was 118kg when I first visited the clinic pre surgery, my current weight 9 months later is 77kg. I have achieved a level of fitness that I would have never believed possible I am now down to 9% body fat. The surgery is not a magic bullet, you have to put the work in, but boy oh boy is it worth it! I cannot recommend these guys enough.

Thank you so much to the whole Auckland Surgical Associates team. From me and my family.

~ Jeremy

Weight loss surgery is a life-changing decision — and the journey doesn't end in the operating room. Our support groups are designed to help you navigate life after surgery with confidence, encouragement, and a community that understands.

Run as part of a comprehensive 5-year follow-up programme, our monthly support groups bring together patients at every stage of their post-surgery journey. Whether you had your procedure last month or last year, you are always welcome.

{{/* ===== 4. WHAT TO EXPECT ===== */}}

What to expect

A problem shared is a problem halved.

Our support groups follow a semi-structured format, facilitated by Amanda, our psychologist. Each session is a safe, welcoming space where you can share your experiences, ask questions, and hear from others who are on the same journey.

Sessions typically include:

  • Celebrating progress — acknowledging milestones and wins, big and small
  • Peer support — hearing from others who understand the challenges and triumphs of life after surgery
  • Open discussion — a chance to ask questions, raise concerns, and share tips
  • Normalising the journey — understanding that ups and downs are a natural part of the process

Every participant contributes to the group — your experience and insights help others, just as theirs will help you.

{{/* ===== 5. HOW TO JOIN ===== */}}

How to join

Support groups are available to all Auckland Surgical Associates bariatric surgery patients and commence after your procedure. You can also request to be placed on the pre-surgery list so you are ready to join as soon as you recover.

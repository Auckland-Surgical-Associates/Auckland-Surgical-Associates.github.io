Conditions that benefit from weight loss treatment

Both medical and surgical approaches can be effective for patients living with:

{{/* Blood drop icon — diabetes */}}

Type 2 Diabetes

Weight loss can lead to significant improvement or complete resolution of Type 2 diabetes, often reducing or eliminating the need for medication.

Obstructive Sleep Apnoea

Weight loss significantly reduces the severity of sleep apnoea, with many patients no longer requiring CPAP therapy.

Metabolic Syndrome

A cluster of conditions including high blood pressure, elevated blood sugar, and abnormal cholesterol levels that increase cardiovascular risk.

{{/* Person with pain/mood — figure with cloud */}}

Musculoskeletal Pain & Depression

Significant weight loss reduces joint stress and has been shown to improve anxiety, depression, and overall quality of life.

Your Weight Loss Options

We offer two complementary approaches to obesity treatment. Many patients benefit from a combination of both.

Medical Weight Loss

Non-surgical, medication-based approaches combined with dietitian and psychology support. Suitable for patients who prefer to avoid surgery or who may not yet qualify for a surgical procedure.

  • Non-invasive - no surgery or recovery time
  • Reversible - can stop treatment at any time
  • Lower upfront cost
  • Suitable for patients with lower BMI (27.5+)
  • Can be combined with surgical approaches

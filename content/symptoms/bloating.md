## Bloating

Bloating is a sensation of fullness or tightness in the abdomen, often accompanied by visible swelling. It can develop from excess gas, poor digestion, or fluid buildup in the digestive tract.

**Common Causes:**
- Overeating
- Eating too quickly
- Swallowing air while eating or drinking
- Consuming gas-producing foods
- Digestive disorders like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS)
- Lactose intolerance
- Celiac disease
- Hormonal fluctuations during menstruation or pregnancy

**Symptoms** may include abdominal discomfort, cramping, and a feeling of heaviness.

**Treatment** varies depending on the underlying cause and may include dietary changes, lifestyle modifications, over-the-counter medications, or prescription drugs.

Chronic or severe bloating accompanied by other concerning symptoms should be evaluated by a healthcare professional to rule out serious conditions.

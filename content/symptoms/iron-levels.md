## Symptoms of Iron Deficiency:

- Fatigue and weakness
- Pale skin and nail beds
- Shortness of breath
- Dizziness or lightheadedness
- Cold hands and feet
- Brittle nails
- Headaches
- Rapid heartbeat or palpitations

## Treatment Options:

**Iron supplements:** Prescribed by a healthcare provider, typically taken orally or can be given by intramuscular or intravenous route

**Dietary changes:** Consuming iron-rich foods such as red meat, poultry, fish, beans, lentils, spinach, and fortified cereals.

**Vitamin C consumption:** Enhances iron absorption when consumed alongside iron-rich foods or supplements.

**Intravenous (IV) iron therapy:** In severe cases or when oral supplements are not effective, iron may be administered intravenously.

**Blood transfusion:** Reserved for critical cases with significant blood loss or severe anaemia.

Regular monitoring of iron levels and adherence to treatment are crucial for managing iron deficiency effectively.

**Definition:** Surgical weight loss procedure.

**Types:** Gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, duodenal switch.

**Purpose:** Treat severe obesity, related health issues.

**Criteria:** BMI 40+ or BMI 30+ with health problems.

**Procedure:** Laparoscopic under general anaesthetic

**Risks:** Infection, anastomotic/staple line leaks, nutritional deficiencies.

**Benefits:** Weight loss, improved health, quality and length of life.

**Lifestyle:** Diet, exercise, psychological support.

**Follow-Up:** Regular monitoring, adherence to changes.

**Considerations:** Major decision, a commitment to improving health and lifestyle.

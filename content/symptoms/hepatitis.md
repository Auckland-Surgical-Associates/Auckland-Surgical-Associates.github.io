## Symptoms of Hepatitis:

- Fatigue
- Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
- Abdominal pain
- Loss of appetite
- Nausea and vomiting
- Dark urine
- Pale stools
- Joint pain
- Fever
- Clay-colored bowel movements

## Treatment options for Hepatitis:

- Antiviral medications (for viral hepatitis)
- Interferon injections (for viral hepatitis)
- Liver transplant (in severe cases)
- Rest and adequate nutrition
- Avoidance of alcohol and certain medications
- Regular medical monitoring and follow-up
- Vaccination (for hepatitis A and B)
- Good hygiene practices to prevent transmission
- Supportive care to manage symptoms and complications

## Preventive Measures:

- Vaccination against hepatitis A and B
- Practicing safe sex and using protection
- Avoiding sharing needles or other drug paraphernalia
- Practicing good hygiene, including frequent handwashing
- Avoiding consuming contaminated food or water
- Seeking medical care promptly if exposed to hepatitis or experiencing symptoms

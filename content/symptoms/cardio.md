## Pre-Bariatric Surgery Benefits

- **Enhances Surgical Outcomes:** Regular cardio helps reduce body fat and shrink liver size, which can improve the safety and efficacy of the surgical procedure.

- **Improves Cardiac Health:** Cardiovascular exercise strengthens the heart, which can help manage high blood pressure and reduce the risk of heart disease associated with obesity.

- **Increases Lung Capacity:** Cardio activities like walking or cycling enhance lung function, which can be beneficial for surgery and recovery.

- **Boosts Metabolism:** Regular physical activity increases metabolic rate, aiding in faster and more effective weight loss before surgery.

- **Reduces Surgical Risks:** Lowering overall body weight through cardio can decrease the risk of complications during anesthesia and surgery.

- **Enhances Psychological Well-being:** Exercise releases endorphins, improving mood and reducing stress, which is crucial for patients facing major surgery.

## Post-Bariatric Surgery Benefits

- **Supports Weight Loss Maintenance:** Cardio exercise helps maintain higher metabolic rates post-surgery, aiding in continued weight loss and prevention of weight regain.

- **Improves Cardiovascular Health:** Regular cardio post-surgery continues to reduce risks of heart diseases and strengthens the cardiovascular system.

- **Increases Insulin Sensitivity:** Exercise after surgery can improve insulin sensitivity, which is especially beneficial for patients with Type 2 diabetes.

- **Promotes Healthy Habits:** Establishing a routine of regular exercise helps in developing a long-term healthy lifestyle, crucial for the success of bariatric surgery.

- **Enhances Mood and Energy Levels:** Regular physical activity helps improve overall mood, reduce depression and anxiety, and boost energy levels, helping individuals adapt to new lifestyles.

- **Improves Physical Functionality:** As weight decreases, mobility typically increases, making it easier and more comfortable to engage in various physical activities.

Cardio exercises both before and after bariatric surgery are key components of a comprehensive approach to weight loss, contributing to both physical and mental health improvements.

**Symptoms of Ulcers:**

- Burning or gnawing pain in the upper abdomen, typically between meals or at night
- Nausea or vomiting
- Bloating
- Feeling full quickly
- Unexplained weight loss
- Indigestion
- Heartburn
- Dark or bloody stools
- Fatigue

**Treatment:**

**Medications:**

- Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) to reduce stomach acid production
- Antibiotics to kill H. pylori bacteria (if present)
- Antacids to neutralize stomach acid

**Lifestyle changes:**

- Avoiding spicy, acidic, or fatty foods
- Quitting smoking
- Limiting alcohol consumption
- Managing stress through relaxation techniques or therapy
- Avoiding non steroidal anti-inflammatory medications

Prompt medical attention is crucial if symptoms worsen or if there is vomiting blood, difficulty breathing, or severe abdominal pain.

**Vitamin A Overview:**
- Fat-soluble nutrient vital for vision, immune system, cell growth, and reproduction.

**Forms:**
- Preformed (animal sources) and provitamin A carotenoids (plant sources).

**Function:**
- Essential for vision, immune health, cell growth, and reproduction.

**Sources:**
- Animal: Liver, fish, dairy; Plant: Carrots, spinach, sweet potatoes.

**Deficiency:**
- Night blindness, immune dysfunction, xerophthalmia.

**Toxicity:**
- Hypervitaminosis A with symptoms like nausea, liver damage.

**Recommendations:**
- Vary by age and gender; avoid excess during pregnancy.

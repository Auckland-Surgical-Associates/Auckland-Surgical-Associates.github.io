**Vitamin A:**

Symptoms: Night blindness, dry eyes, skin issues, impaired immune function.

Treatment: Increase consumption of foods rich in vitamin A such as carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach, and liver. Consider supplements under medical supervision.

**Vitamin D:**

Symptoms: Fatigue, bone pain, muscle weakness, depression.

Treatment: Get regular sunlight exposure, consume fortified foods like milk, fatty fish, and egg yolks. Supplements may be prescribed by a healthcare professional or accessed via taking some multivitamins or sourcing from a health food shop

**Vitamin K:**

Symptoms: Excessive bleeding, easy bruising, weakened bones.

Treatment: Consume leafy greens, cruciferous vegetables, and fermented foods. Supplements may be necessary, especially for those on blood-thinning medications.

**Vitamin E:**

Symptoms: Muscle weakness, vision problems, impaired immune function.

Treatment: Increase intake of nuts, seeds, and vegetable oils. Supplements may be recommended for individuals with certain health conditions.

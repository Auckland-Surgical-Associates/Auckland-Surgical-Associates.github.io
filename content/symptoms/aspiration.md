Aspiration is inhaling foreign substances into the lungs.

- Causes include gastro esophageal reflux, swallowing difficulties or impaired consciousness.

**Symptoms:** coughing, difficulty breathing, chest pain.

**Risks:** advanced age, neurological conditions.

- Prompt recognition and management are crucial.

## Lymphoedema

Lymphoedema is the accumulation of lymph(clear fluid) in the bodily tissues generally under the outer layer of the skin.

It commonly appears in weight-dependent areas such as the lower legs and is frequently associated with conditions including obesity, immobility, and varicose veins.

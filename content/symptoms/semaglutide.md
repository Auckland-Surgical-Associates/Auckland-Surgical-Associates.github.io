**Semaglutide** is described as "a weekly injected medication for the treatment of type 2 diabetes and obesity."

## Symptoms

The medication can cause:
- Nausea
- Vomiting
- Diarrhoea
- Abdominal pain
- Decreased appetite

These symptoms "often improve over time but should be monitored closely."

## Treatment Approaches

Management strategies include:
- Starting with a lower dose and gradually increasing it
- Taking the medication with meals
- Staying hydrated
- Using over-the-counter anti-nausea medications under healthcare provider guidance

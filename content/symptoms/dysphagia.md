**Symptoms:**
- Difficulty in swallowing
- Chest pain
- Regurgitation

**Treatment:**
- Treatment involves treating the cause of dysphagia and altering the diet accordingly

Difficulty in swallowing may be due to obstruction, reflux, neurological, muscular and connective tissue disorders.

## General

### Is there parking available?

Yes, we have plenty of parking outside the front entrance with level access.

### Do I need a referral?

You do not need a referral, but we may request access to some information from your GP or specialists.

### I live out of Auckland; can I do a remote consult?

The clinic accommodates out-of-town patients through phone consultations, though in-person visits are preferred. Patients should complete blood tests and questionnaires before remote appointments.

### Do you offer medical weight loss solutions

Yes, we would be delighted to help with this. We have a multidisciplinary team and subspecialist metabolic and bariatric experts who can help.

### How much is a consultation?

Consultations vary between $189 and $379, depending on which specialist you are seeing and for how long.


### When do I have to pay?

Payment occurs on consultation day, with surgery payments required minimum 7 days before the procedure.

### How can I pay?

Accepted methods include EFTPOS, bank transfers, and credit cards. Credit cards aren't accepted for surgery, and American Express isn't accepted at all.

### What is the follow up care after Bariatric surgery?

We offer long term and comprehensive follow up for our patients to ensure excellent outcomes. We offer regular follow up with our dietitian, surgeon and nurse up to 5 years after surgery, plus access to monthly support sessions.

### What are the other costs involved?

Patients cover prescription medications, multivitamins, and Optifast drinks post-discharge.

## Surgery

### Where will my surgery be?

Dr Suheelan Kulasegaran and Dr Michael Booth operate privately at Southern Cross North Harbour and Allevia Auckland City. 

### What preparation do I have to do for surgery?

Follow pre-operative diet plans (Optifast), undergo medical tests, attend consultations, quit smoking, and avoid certain medications as advised.

### How many appointments do I need pre surgery?

Multiple appointments include surgeon consultation, two dietitian sessions, psychological assessment, and final consent meeting with surgeon.

### How long do I have to do the Optifast diet for before surgery?

Typically 2-4 weeks before surgery, customised based on individual factors and surgeon recommendations.

## Recovery & Aftercare

### When can I return to work?

Most patients can expect to return to work within 1-2 weeks after bariatric surgery, depending on the type of procedure performed and individual recovery.

### When can I travel post-surgery?

Wait 2-4 weeks minimum before non-essential travel, though personalised surgeon guidance is recommended.

### What sort of exercise should I do pre- and post-surgery?

Pre-surgery: focus on low-impact activities like walking and swimming. Post-surgery: start gently and gradually intensify with professional guidance.

### What are the best foods to eat post-surgery?

Emphasise protein-rich foods (lean meats, fish, eggs, dairy, legumes), gradually increase fruits and vegetables, avoid high-calorie/sugar/fat foods, and stay hydrated.

### How long after surgery can I consider pregnancy?

Wait 12-18 months post-surgery for body stabilisation. Discuss plans with healthcare providers for proper monitoring.

### What support do you offer post-surgery?

The clinic provides thorough 5-year follow-up, answers questions, and hosts popular bi-monthly support groups.
